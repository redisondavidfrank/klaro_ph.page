About Us

Klaro is an alternative rock band from Puerto Princesa City. They began their musical journey in 2022, driven by passion and an undying will to be heard. Their genuine love for music and compassion propel them to make heads jam with their alternative rock vibes. The band still continues to venture out shows, different music, write songs, hoping to someday prove their talent
